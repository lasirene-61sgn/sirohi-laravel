HOW TO TEST CUSTOMER BUSINESS API IN POSTMAN:

1. FIRST API: Get Business Names
   URL: http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/customer/business-names
   Method: GET
   Headers:
   - Accept: application/json
   - Authorization: Bearer {your_sanctum_token}

2. SECOND API: Get Customers by Business Name
   URL: http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/customer/customers-by-business?business_name={business_name}
   Method: GET
   Headers:
   - Accept: application/json
   - Authorization: Bearer {your_sanctum_token}
   Query Parameter:
   - business_name: The name of the business you want to filter by

EXAMPLE:
To get all customers with business name "ABC Company":
http://127.0.0.1:8000/api/customer/customers-by-business?business_name=ABC Company

REQUIREMENTS:
- You need a valid Sanctum authentication token
- The customer must belong to an admin
- Only customers from the same admin will be returned
